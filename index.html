Our Mission

We build Modular.

Every piece is a part of the system. Leveraging our digital design system ACM®, we insist upon a modular approach between all layers of the construction process. This brings us absolute detachability for extended reuse, adaptability for embracing uncertainty, and scalability to adopt all lifestyle changes.

We build Circular.

Earth and it's materials should belong to all of us. We meticulously design every component to be maintained, reused, or processed individually. Neustrukt houses will be taken care of diligently throughout it's lifetime giving both the owner or the renter peace of mind.

We build Smart.

We believe smart home features should be about longevity and health, or in other words serving towards the inhabitants comfort.

Neustrukt houses will constantly observe and log data like air quality to provide unprecedented life standards.

We build the Future.

We need a radical change on our view of construction and housing. We need more collaborations, regulations and invidual effort for adoption.
